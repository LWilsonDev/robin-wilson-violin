Intro

The violin has such a lot to offer - it is versatile and fun to learn, with a rich history and repertoire drawn from the World's greatest composers. ​​

I am a professional violinist based in Tunbridge Wells, regularly performing with top London Orchestras as well as offering one-to-one tuition.

My lessons are focused on enjoyment, with a goal-driven approach to help students achieve their full potential through exploring repertoire, theory, song and movement. I am able to offer bespoke lessons for advanced students, and work on detailed aspects of technique, repertoire, and exam preparation. I am equally happy to teach beginners and less experienced players.

Whether you are looking for a teacher for your child, or hoping to develop your own playing, please don't hesitate to get in touch and I will be happy to answer any questions and arrange a free trial lesson.
